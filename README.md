This project is an AI-powered system designed to monitor the health of satellites and protect them from external threats such as asteroids. It continuously analyzes real-time satellite data to detect anomalies, predict potential failures, and identify collision risks. By combining artificial intelligence with space monitoring, the system helps ensure safe, reliable, and uninterrupted satellite operations.

Key Features
~AI-based detection of anomalies in satellite health
~Real-time monitoring of satellite parameters like position and status
~Asteroid tracking and prediction of possible collisions
~Alert system for potential risks and failures
~Data analysis to improve mission safety and decision-making

How It Works
~Collects real-time satellite data from sensors
~Uses AI models to analyze system health
~Identifies unusual patterns or failures
~Tracks nearby asteroids and evaluates risk
~Generates alerts or preventive actions to avoid damage

Objective
~To improve satellite safety using AI
~To predict failures before they occur
~To prevent collisions with space objects
~To ensure smooth and efficient satellite operations
